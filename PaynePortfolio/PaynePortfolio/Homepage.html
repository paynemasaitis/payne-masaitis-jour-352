My name is Payne Masaitis...

...and I am an aspiring sports journalist and graphic designer at the University of Maryland's Philip Merrill College of Journalism.


I am a Baltimore-based writer and content creator that graduated from Archbishop Curley High School, where I was a member of the National Honors Society. My journalistic focus is on sports media, which I pursue through my current writing position at Eutaw Street Report and Russell Street Report. I deliver near-daily Orioles articles, which blend statistics and storytelling through player analysis, game previews and recaps, team news and much more.

Alongside my writing, I have also pursued my passion of graphic design. I develop visual content for my Instagram page, PCMGraphics, where I transform statistics and news into impactful NFL graphics. The page was established in January 2026, marking a new beginning in my media career. Through both my journalism and content creation projects, I aim to balance creativity with analysis while also telling stories with integrity and accessibility.
